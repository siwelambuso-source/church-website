📅 CHURCH CALENDAR 2026

VISION 2037

Mpolonjeni Alliance Church has announced ambitious plans to construct a new state-of-the-art auditorium to accommodate its rapidly growing congregation and to serve as a center for community outreach and Christian-based events. Located in a fast-developing area, the church has seen steady growth over the years, with its current building no longer meeting the needs of its expanding membership. In response, the church leadership has unveiled Vision 2037, a long-term project that aims to complete a modern auditorium designed to host 1,500 worshipers.

Extending the current church structure to accommodate more families, expand our ministries, and create a greater impact in our community. Vision 2037 also seeks to provide a home for international brethren who can join us through our live streaming services. The vision is not only aimed at touching local souls but even from all corners of the world. We believe God is calling us to enlarge our tent and build a legacy of faith for generations to come.

Enlarge the place of your tent... — Isaiah 54:2

Strategic Pillar 1: Evangelism

Taking the Gospel beyond our walls through open-air crusades, door-to-door outreach, community evangelism, and digital ministry. We aim to reach 5,000 souls with the message of Christ by 2037, establishing new believers in their faith through discipleship and follow-up programs.

5,000 Souls Target 100+ Discipleship Groups

Strategic Pillar 2: Social Impact

Demonstrating God's love through practical action. Developing community-based programs that extend the church's presence beyond its walls, establishing partnerships with other faith-based organizations engaged in social projects, and running initiatives that provide support to those in need.

10,000+ Meals Yearly 500+ Children Supported

Church Auditorium Extension Project

Expanding our current sanctuary to accommodate more worshippers, with enhanced facilities for children's ministry, youth programs, and community gatherings. In the short term, the church is focusing on building classrooms and a kitchen, while the larger auditorium project will unfold progressively with an expected completion by 2037.
